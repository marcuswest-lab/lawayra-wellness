about LaWayra

LaWayra is a retreat center in the mountains of Colombia, surrounded by nature and panoramic views. The land offers places to rest, including hammocks, a river, a pool, and a sauna.

Yoga and meditation take place in the maloka, a large wooden ceremonial space used for movement, meditation, and ritual. People come from around the world to spend time in both personal reflection and community.

LaWayra hosts wellness retreats with experienced, international facilitators and guides, creating a space for practical learning and spiritual exploration.

Guests are supported through reflection, creative practices, shared meals, and embodied movement.

about our retreats

Our wellness retreats include silent mornings, yoga and movement practices, breathwork, guided meditation, and teachings to support a personal meditation practice. The approach draws from Eastern traditions, Indigenous Colombian wisdom, and modern spiritual practice.

Time is shared between individual reflection and group workshops, often alongside additional healers and creative practices. Meals are prepared daily by local chefs, and accommodations range from shared dorms to private cabins with comfortable amenities.

our team

Our retreats are led by experienced, international facilitators who bring a wide range of specialties. Lead guide Brigitte Kolibab has been teaching yoga and meditation for 13 years, facilitating community spaces across the globe with an intuitive ability to meet you where you are.

Through connections with healers in India, Indonesia, the United States, Italy, Turkey, and Colombia, our team offers breathwork, massage, reiki and energy work, sound healing, cacao ceremonies, and yoga practices including Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Ashtanga. We also host collaboration retreats with visiting facilitators who bring fresh perspectives and teachings.
